nice to meet you
EastWest Collective is a small group of moonlighting makers based in San Luis Obispo. We began the collective to strengthen the maker community here in the Central Coast and believe in the importance of giving back, donating a portion of our sales to local nonprofits.

Join us as we travel around California
sharing the joy that handmade objects bring to our lives.

Van Doan
Prior to the Central Coast life, he lived in Brooklyn and worked at a publishing house in Manhattan. After another brutal winter, he shipped several boxes to the West, packed his Suby to the gills, and spent 4 months zig-zagging across the U.S. and British Columbia, from one climbing destination to another, meeting old friends and making new ones.

If he's not in one of the area's ceramic studios, then most likely he can be found at the climbing gym or on a rock somewhere with friends.
